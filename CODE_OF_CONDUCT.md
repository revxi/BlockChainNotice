# Code of Conduct

### BlockChainNotice -- Decentralised Notice and File Sharing System

## 1. Introduction

This Code of Conduct outlines the standards of behavior expected from
all contributors, collaborators, and stakeholders involved in the
BlockChainNotice project. The goal is to foster a professional, ethical,
and respectful environment conducive to high‑quality academic and
technical work.

## 2. Professional Conduct Expectations

### 2.1 Maintain Professionalism

-   Demonstrate integrity, honesty, and responsibility in all
    contributions.
-   Engage in constructive and respectful communication.
-   Provide well‑reasoned, evidence‑based feedback.

### 2.2 Encourage Collaboration

-   Support open discussion, contribution, and idea‑sharing.
-   Respect differing technical opinions and approaches.
-   Credit others' work appropriately.

### 2.3 Uphold Technical Quality

-   Follow the established project structure, coding standards, and
    documentation guidelines.
-   Submit only original, verifiable, and rigorously tested work.
-   Ensure that contributions align with the project's goals and
    engineering best practices.

## 3. Unacceptable Behavior

### 3.1 Interpersonal Misconduct

-   Harassment, discrimination, or derogatory comments based on
    identity, background, or beliefs.
-   Personal attacks, hostility, or disrespectful language.

### 3.2 Academic and Ethical Violations

-   Plagiarism or misrepresentation of contributions.
-   Sharing confidential or restricted information without
    authorization.

### 3.3 Technical Misconduct

-   Submitting intentionally harmful or malicious code.
-   Introducing security vulnerabilities or unauthorized access
    mechanisms.
-   Disruption of workflow, productivity, or collaboration.

## 4. Responsibilities of Project Maintainers

-   Review contributions objectively and fairly.
-   Enforce guidelines consistently and transparently.
-   Remove or reject contributions that violate the Code of Conduct.
-   Take corrective measures when necessary, including warnings or
    removal from the project.

## 5. Reporting Procedure

Participants may report violations to project maintainers. Reports
should include: - A clear description of the concern. - Relevant
evidence or context, if available.

All reports will be handled confidentially and professionally.

## 6. Enforcement and Resolution

Violations may result in: - A formal warning - Temporary suspension from
contributions - Removal from the project - Escalation to academic
supervisors where applicable

## 7. Applicability

This Code of Conduct applies to all communication channels,
contributions, documentation, and activities related to the
BlockChainNotice project.

## 8. Acknowledgment

By contributing to this project, participants agree to uphold the
standards outlined in this Code of Conduct.
